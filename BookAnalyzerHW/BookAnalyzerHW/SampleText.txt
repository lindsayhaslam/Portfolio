Title: The Greatest Book of All Time

Author: Lindsay Haslam

Release Date: January 21, 2023

There once was a girl named Lindsay, who was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah. She isn't very tall but she isn't short, either. She loves colors, cooking, and hanging out with her loved ones. If she could be any animal in the world, Lindsay would be a sea turtle. Her favorite season is Spring and loves foggy or rainy weather. Lindsay's go-to place to go on vacation is New York City. Lindsay's favorite movies are Legally Blonde, Crazy Rich Asians, and the Imitation Game. She is married and owns an adorable little puppy named Rocky. This year, Lindsay is trying to get into running and wants to run a 5K next summer.
